A classic booster, mildly modified from the ubiquitous Electro-Harmonix LPB-1 Linear Power Booster. This pedal can be used to girth up your clean sound, or smack the front end of an amp or drive pedal for more gain and harmonics. Turn gritty amps into chunky overdrive monsters, turn overdriven amps into wailing feedback dragons, kick your overdrive in the plums and watch it saturate into a fuzztone. The possibilities really are endless and the Saltbooster is packed full of Salty power to cater to all your boost needs.

Features

  • Alpha pots
  • Neutrik jacks
  • True bypass via 3PDT switch
  • Boss style 2.1mm centre negative barrel type DC jack
  • Metal bezled LED(LED colours negotiable)
  • Controls - Boost
  • 2-year guarantee
