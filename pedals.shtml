Pedals

Salt Booster
A classic booster. This pedal can be used to girth up your clean sound, or smack the front end of an amp or drive pedal for more gain and harmonics.
Green Muff
Based on the Russian Sovtek Green Big Muff. Loved by bass players and guitarists alike, this is one of the most popular Muff designs ever produced.
Salt Shaker
A simple yet versatile tremolo sporting both modern and vintage style sounds.
Salt Storm
A vintage style crumbly overdrive. The Salt Storm has a garagey, punky dirt sound.
Salt Breaker
A Salty overdrive pedal with a whole whack-load of tweakability. Set the basic sound with the three trimpots inside the pedal, and then enjoy being able to sweep the tone control across it's entire range to produce a bunch of tasty tones
Fuzz Face
A modern take on a vintage classic. This version of the Fuzz Face uses germanium PNP transistors like the originals but has extra controls for versatility.
